Managing Ride Intensity

Including lots of turns and drops in a ride may make it thrilling but there is a difference between an exciting ride and a painful ride. If the intensity is too high, your passengers will experience a lot of discomfort and may not ride at all. However, you don't need to avoid sharp turns or loops if you build them right.
