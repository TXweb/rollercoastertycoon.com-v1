OFFICIAL ADD-ON PACKS

The are two Official add-on packs for Roller Coaster Tycoon:

Description: Stretch your imagination with New Loopy Landscapes Expansion Pack. From Rocket Ships and Medieval Castles to Winter Wonderlands and new environments, you now have a complete working palette to create your next thrill-a-minute masterpiece. Build on new landscapes, provide more shops and stalls or take on one of 30 new scenarios filled with new exciting challenges. It's all here and it's waiting for you! This expansion two pack also includes Corkscrew Follies. NOTE: This is an add-on pack and requires a copy of the main RollerCoaster Tycoon game to play. (It will work with any size game installation).

Screenshots: Coming Soon

Technical Spec:

  • Platform: PC only
  • Minimum: P90MHz, 16Mb RAM, 4XCD, 50MB hard disk, 1MB SVGA card
  • Recommended (for good game performance): P200MHz, 32Mb RAM, 8XCD, 50MB hard disk, 2MB accelerated SVGA card
  • Ideal (for ultimate game performance): PII 350MHz, 64Mb RAM, 8xCD, 180MB hard disk, 4MB accelerated SVGA card
  • Note: All specs require Windows 95/98, Windows 95 compatible Sound Card, DirectX 5.0 (included on CD) and Mouse. Does not need 3D support or accessories.

US only
