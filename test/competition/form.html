Your upload questions answered

Q. How do I play?
A. Read the game rules for more information.

Q. Can I play if I do not own the RollerCoaster Tycoon CD-ROM game?
A. No. The game is required in order to use the special competition scenarios.

Q. Can I upload my track file instead of the special competition scenario file?
A. No. Only entries that use the special competition scenario will be accepted. The scenario can be downloaded from the Web site.

Q. Why do I need to test the ride first before uploading it?
A. The first criteria to win the contest is to be a very exciting ride. The ride's excitement rating is only calculated when the ride is run in its surroundings. If it isn't tested, then the statistics are not calculated and we can not see which are the most exciting. See the main competition page for more information.

Uploading your scenario indicates your agreement of the following:

  • The track designs used in the competition scenarios may be made available for download by others via this Web site or any other distribution means. No profit will be made from these distributions by Hasbro Interactive.
  • Other Web sites will not be allowed to profit from distributing track designs generated from the competition scenarios.
  • Ownership of the scenarios and their contents passes to Hasbro Interactive.
  • Any offensive names entered into the site during upload will result in all scenarios by that creator being removed and further uploads from the offender being barred.
