  18. The 1st place winner will receive a RollerCoaster book, a RollerCoaster Tycoon mousemat and a RollerCoaster Tycoon poster. 2nd and 3rd place winners receive a RollerCoaster Tycoon mousemat and poster.
  19. The odds of a player winning will be one in the number of correct entries received during the competition's length.
