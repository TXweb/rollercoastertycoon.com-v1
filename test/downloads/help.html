  1. Download the zip file and extract the two files to their hard disk. One file is the track design and the other is the preview photo image.
     
  2. Double click on either of the files and the track and photo will both be installed and the game will start running.

An alternative to step 2 is to copy the track and photo files directly into the Tracks directory (a sub-directory of the RollerCoaster Tycoon main directory). This can be useful when installing lots of rides as it means the game does not start after each ride is installed.

Q. Sometimes I can't I access the extra track designs I have installed. Why?
A. New tracks will only be available if the ride type is available in the scenario you wish to build in. For example, if a mine train coaster is not available in your scenario and you have installed a mine train coasters track, you will not be able to use it until the mine train coaster is available.

Q. How do I get preview photos for other, unofficial rides I have downloaded?
A. Only official tracks have preview photos with them.
