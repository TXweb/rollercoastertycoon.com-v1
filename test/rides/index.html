RIDE EXCHANGE

You've arrived at the Ride Exchange - the place to swap rides with RollerCoaster Tycoon fans worldwide.

THE RIDE EXCHANGE IS CURRENTLY BEING OVERHAULED AND WILL BE OPERATIONAL SHORTLY. WE APOLOGIZE FOR ANY INCONVENIENCE.

The Ride Exchange proved to be overwhelmingly popular with over 19,000 rides being submitted in two months. As a result, this service has been in the process of being upgraded and this work has been seriously delayed for several reasons. Unfortunately, this has meant that it has not been available whilst the work was carried out.

However, coding of the new system has begun. A date for it's re-launch will be communicated via this web site as soon as more solid timings are known.

We apologize for this necessary break in service and I hope you will find the revised Ride Exchange will be worth the wait.

Webmaster - 26 Sept 2000.
