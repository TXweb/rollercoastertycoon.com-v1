RULES FOR TREASURE HUNT

  1. This site is operated and controlled by Hasbro Interactive whose address for the purposes of this competition is 2 Roundwood Avenue, Stockley Park, Uxbridge, Middlesex, UB11 1AZ, UK.
  2. By using this site and entering this competition, you signify your agreement to these rules.
  3. Only one entry per person is allowed.
  4. Only one entry will be accepted from a single e-mail address.
  5. Employees of Hasbro Interactive and its affiliated companies and family members of such employees are not eligible to participate in the competition.
  6. The competition will begin on 15th February 2000 and end on 11th April 2000 (02:00 am PST).
  7. Entries must be received by Hasbro Interactive no later than the competition end date noted above. Hasbro Interactive is not responsible for entries that are late, corrupted, or lost due to technical difficulties.
  8. No purchase is required to enter this competition.
  9. Due to locally imposed restrictions, only those people who are currently resident in United Kingdom, United States of America, and France are eligible to participate in the competition. Entries from persons not resident in these countries will be void. Other countries may be added during the course of the contest without notice.
  10. If entrants are under a minimum age, we must notify the entrant's parents to let them know that the entrants e-mail address has been submitted to us. If the parents wish, they can ask us to withdraw the entrant's e-mail address and name from our list. If we are unable to send this notification, the entry will be void.
  11. US entrants aged under 13 years of age as at 11th April 2000 will be void due to limits on data collection.
  12. No cash alternative for prizes will be available.
  13. The winner will be drawn at random from all the entrants that have completed the treasure hunt successfully and filled in the entry form.
  14. Winners will be notified via e-mail up to two weeks following the end date of the competition.
  15. The judge is Hasbro Interactive or its designee and the judge's decision will be final.
  16. Hasbro Interactive reserves the right to change these rules at any time without prior notice by posting the amended rules onto this web site.
  17. European entrants please note that information you submit when entering the competition will be transferred outside the European Economic Area for the purposes of processing by Hasbro Interactive and its affiliated companies, but only for the purposes of running this competition. Unless you indicate your desire to be included on Hasbro Interactive's e-mail mailing list, information you enter will be deleted at or shortly after the end of the competition.
  18. Any legal issues concerning this contest will be dealt with according to English Law and by the English Courts.
  19. The selected winners will receive a RollerCoaster Tycoon mousemat.
  20. The odds of a player winning will be five in the number of correct entries received during the competition's length.
